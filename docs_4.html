Getting Started

Welcome to Reliqt

Reliqt is a Real World Asset (RWA) collectibles platform built on Base. We tokenize PSA-graded physical trading cards into onchain tokens — each card gets its own ERC-20 token, its own liquidity pool on Uniswap V4, and a native trading pair with $RELIQT.

Overview

How It Works

Reliqt bridges the physical collectibles market with DeFi. Here's the complete lifecycle of a card on the platform:

1
Physical Card Acquisition
We source PSA 10-graded trading cards — the highest possible grade — from verified sellers. Only Gem Mint certified cards enter the Reliqt ecosystem, spanning Sports, Pokémon, and Anime categories.
2
Vault & Insurance
Every physical card is placed in a secure, fully-insured vault. The vault ensures the underlying asset is safe, protected, and independently verified at all times.
3
Tokenization on Base
Each card is tokenized into a unique ERC-20 token deployed on Base. A LeBron James PSA 10 becomes rLEBRON. The token supply is fixed and represents fractional ownership of the physical card.
4
Liquidity Pool on Uniswap V4
A dedicated liquidity pool is created on Uniswap V4 for each card token, paired with $RELIQT. This enables permissionless, real-time price discovery and trading for every card.
5
Trade, Hold, or Provide Liquidity
Users can buy or sell card tokens using $RELIQT via the Reliqt Trade interface or directly on Uniswap V4. All activity is onchain, transparent, and non-custodial.
Non-custodial. Reliqt never holds your tokens. Once you buy a card token, it lives in your own wallet — not on a platform database.

Platform

Trading & Pairs

MARKETPLACE

The Reliqt Trade interface provides a full candlestick chart and order interface for every card token. All trading happens onchain via Uniswap V4 — no order books, no centralized matching.

How to Trade

1
Connect Wallet
Connect your Base-compatible wallet (Coinbase Wallet, MetaMask, or any Privy-supported wallet) at reliqt.app.
2
Get $RELIQT
Acquire $RELIQT on Uniswap V4 (Base). $RELIQT is required to buy any card token on the platform.
3
Select a Card
Go to the Trade page, select a card token (e.g. rLEBRON), and review the live chart, market cap, supply, and last value.
4
Buy or Sell
Enter the amount of $RELIQT to spend (Buy) or card tokens to sell. Approve the transaction in your wallet. Tokens land in your wallet instantly.

Help

FAQ

Common questions about Reliqt, $RELIQT, and the platform.

What is Reliqt?
Reliqt is a Real World Asset (RWA) collectibles platform on Base. We tokenize PSA 10-graded trading cards into ERC-20 tokens, each with its own liquidity pool on Uniswap V4 paired with $RELIQT.
What is $RELIQT?
$RELIQT is the native token of the Reliqt ecosystem. It is the base currency for all card purchases, gacha spins, and liquidity pairs. Launched via Bankr Bot on Base Mainnet.
Are the cards real?
Yes. Every card on Reliqt is a physical PSA 10 (Gem Mint) graded collectible stored in a fully insured vault. When you buy a card token, you hold fractional ownership of that real physical card.
How do I get $RELIQT?
$RELIQT is available on Uniswap V4 on Base Mainnet. Swap ETH or any Base token for $RELIQT. Once you have it, you can buy card tokens, spin the gacha, or provide liquidity.
Why $RELIQT instead of USDC?
Using $RELIQT as the base pair means every activity on Reliqt directly contributes to the $RELIQT economy — creating a closed-loop ecosystem where platform growth and token value are aligned.
Is Reliqt non-custodial?
Yes. Reliqt never holds your tokens. Once you purchase a card token, it transfers directly to your wallet. You maintain full ownership and control at all times.
What networks does Reliqt support?
Reliqt is deployed exclusively on Base Mainnet — a Layer 2 built on Ethereum offering fast, low-fee transactions ideal for frequent card trading and gacha interactions.
How is gacha fairness guaranteed?
All gacha outcomes are determined by onchain randomness on Base. Results are transparent, verifiable, and cannot be manipulated by Reliqt or any party.
