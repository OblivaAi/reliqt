Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

Reliqt is a decentralized platform. We do not collect personal information beyond your public wallet address. We may collect anonymized usage analytics to improve the platform.

Wallet Data

Your wallet address is public on the blockchain. We use it to display your portfolio, points, and referral data. We do not store private keys.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for session management and performance. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

Third Parties

We use Supabase for database services and Base blockchain for on-chain data. These services have their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any off-chain data associated with your wallet by contacting us. On-chain data is permanent by nature of blockchain technology.
